Powerful Features

Everything you need to stay focused and build better habits

🚫

App Interception

Intelligently intercepts distracting apps and presents challenges before granting access. Never permanently blocks - preserves your autonomy.

✅

Smart Task Management

Create tasks with priorities, categories, and deadlines. View your most important tasks directly on the interception screen.

🎴

Spaced Repetition Flashcards

Built-in SM-2 algorithm flashcard system. Turn distraction moments into learning opportunities with unlock challenges.

🧮

Cognitive Challenges

Math problems, puzzles, and task reviews create active friction that makes you reconsider opening distracting apps.

🔐

Privacy First

All data stored locally on your device. Optional encrypted cloud backup. No accounts required. No tracking.

🌐

Community Templates

Browse and share task templates and flashcard decks with the community. Study plans, workout routines, and more.

⏰

Time Windows & Limits

Set active hours when interception is enabled and daily usage limits for specific apps. Stay in control.

☁️

Encrypted Backup

AES-256 encrypted backup to Google Drive or iCloud. Your data stays private even in the cloud.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Daily morning summaries, task reminders, and bedtime notifications to help you build consistent habits.
